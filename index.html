My name is Mason Bruning, I live in the most Southeastern part of Michigan's lower penninsula. I've lived in metro-Detroit for about 8 years; and not much can change my mind in thinking it's about the best city there is.

About me

I'm a college graduate from Oakland University; in northern metro-Detroit. I graduated with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering, with a major focus in Systems Administration, and interest with skill in theoretical mathematics.

I've worked in the healthcare sector; where I grew an application database that was used just in the IT Architectural Dept., to the source-of-truth for all application and project information in BCBSM. I've worked with open-source technologies to transcribe calls, APIs to handle all traffic within BCBSM Detroit's offices, and as a technical writer, DB associate and Business Analyst for BCBSM's AI tool. In the past year, I've received training from the United States Navy and gained more knowledge and experience in the Customer Service/Help Desk parts of IT.
